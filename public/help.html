Frequently Asked Questions

What is ResuTeX?

ResuTeX is an online resume builder that helps you create professional, ATS-friendly resumes with the classic LaTeX look, without needing to write any code.

Is ResuTeX free?

We offer a free plan that includes all the basic features you need to create and download a great resume. We also have premium plans with advanced features like AI-powered writing assistance and a wider range of templates.

How do I download my resume as a PDF?

In the editor, you will see a "Download PDF" button in the top right of the preview pane. Simply click this button to generate and download your resume.
